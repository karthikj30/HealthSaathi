What HealthSaathi Does

before you see your doctor

From symptom capture to doctor-ready reports — HealthSaathi prepares you completely so you walk into every consultation with confidence.

Guided Symptom Intake

Step-by-step intake flow with progress tracking. Capture symptom timeline, intensity, triggers, and trends with easy-to-use sliders and body-map pinning.

Doctor-Ready Reports

Auto-generates a structured clinical summary, doctor quick-review, and a visit checklist — all ready to share via email, copy, or download in seconds.

Auto Summary Export Checklist

Smart Clinical Insights

Rule-based urgency detection, pattern recognition across visits, food-trigger hints, menstrual cycle correlation for abdominal symptoms, and jargon simplification.

Simple Process

Ready in minutes,
not hours

Four easy steps from sign-up to handing your doctor a perfect report.

1

Create Your Account

Quick signup as a Patient or Doctor. Secure authentication with role-based access and full data privacy.

2

Describe Your Symptoms

Use our guided intake — body-map pinning, pain sliders, timeline events, lifestyle factors, and your fears about the consultation.

3

Get Smart Insights

Receive urgency flags, pattern alerts, and a completeness score before you finalize. AI catches what you might miss.

4

Share with Doctor

One-tap export or email your doctor-ready report. Chat directly, share with family, and track the visit in your calendar.
